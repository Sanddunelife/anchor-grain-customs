Our Story

Every Grain Tells a Story

Anchor Grain Customs started at a workshop table with a stack of scrap oak and a simple idea: the things we put our name on should feel like they belong to us. A desk nameplate isn't just a label — it's a small, steady anchor in a busy workday, a nod to who you are and what you've built.

We hand-select real wood — not veneer, not plastic — and engrave each piece with care, so the grain, the knots, and the warmth of the material shine through your name. Whether it's a gift for a new graduate, a welcome-aboard gesture for a new hire, or a treat for your own desk, we believe personalization turns an ordinary object into something worth keeping.

No two pieces of wood are exactly alike — and neither is anyone's name. That's the whole point.
